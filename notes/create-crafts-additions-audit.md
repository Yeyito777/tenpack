# Create Crafts & Additions audit

Status: **not added yet**.

User concern: electricity in Create is unfamiliar and might not fit Tenpack's slower, physical, faction-logistics philosophy. This document records the first audit before deciding.

Audited artifact:

- Create Crafts & Additions `1.5.10`
- Minecraft `1.21.1` NeoForge
- File checked at `/tmp/tenpack-create-addition-audit/createaddition-1.5.10.jar`
- Source URL: `https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/kU1G12Nn/versions/CP8Lhuwu/createaddition-1.5.10.jar`

## What it is

Create Crafts & Additions is the actual **electricity bridge** addon, unlike Create: Diesel Generators.

- Diesel Generators = oilfields/refining/fuel -> rotational power.
- Crafts & Additions = Forge Energy/electric network <-> Create kinetic rotation.

The headline blocks are:

- `createaddition:electric_motor` — FE -> kinetic rotation.
- `createaddition:alternator` — kinetic rotation -> FE.
- `createaddition:modular_accumulator` — multiblock FE storage.
- `createaddition:connector`, `large_connector`, spools/wires — FE transport.
- `createaddition:portable_energy_interface` — energy transfer to/from moving contraptions.
- `createaddition:rolling_mill` — rods/wires/plates internal processing gate.
- `createaddition:liquid_blaze_burner` — blaze burner with straw, accepts liquid fuels.
- `createaddition:tesla_coil` — item charging and redstone-triggered shock damage.

## Recipe audit highlights

### The good news

The dangerous bridge blocks are already naturally late-ish in Tenpack because they use **mechanical crafting**:

- `createaddition:alternator`
- `createaddition:electric_motor`
- `createaddition:tesla_coil`

Tenpack already gates `create:mechanical_crafter` behind precision/brass era. That means C&A's core electricity bridge does not arrive early unless we weaken mechanical crafter progression.

### The rolling mill

Default recipe:

- andesite casing
- shaft
- andesite alloy
- iron plates

This means rolling mill follows the press/plate era and does not bypass the press by itself. Once built, it adds another way to make rods/wires/plates. That seems acceptable if the pack wants a wire economy.

### Capacitors and connectors are cheap

Examples:

- `createaddition:capacitor` = copper plate + zinc plate + redstone torch.
- small connector = copper rod + andesite alloy + slimeball.
- large connector = high-tier rod + two andesite alloy + slimeball.

Cheap wires/connectors are not automatically bad. The risk is only if they let power distribution replace interesting Create transmission too early. Since there is no useful FE -> rotation before electric motors, cheap wires mostly sit idle until the bridge exists.

### Liquid blaze burner / straw

`createaddition:straw` is made by rolling paper or bamboo. Giving a blaze burner a straw allows liquid fuels by bucket/pipe.

Liquid burning recipes include:

- lava: 20000 burn time
- crude oil: 9600
- diesel: 24000
- gasoline: 24000
- biodiesel: 24000
- biofuel: 24000 and `superheated = true`
- ethanol/plantoil/creosote at lower values

This is a meaningful interaction with Tenpack oil. It can be good because it makes fuel logistics matter even more, but it needs care:

- diesel/gasoline into blaze burners may make oil more valuable, which is good;
- biofuel being superheated may bypass blaze-cake style pressure unless biofuel itself is appropriately hard;
- if liquid burner setup is too convenient, it may make normal blaze burner fuel choices irrelevant.

### Creative Energy

The jar contains creative energy block assets/loot, but no normal survival recipe was found in the quick recipe scan. Still verify in JEI if added.

## Philosophy fit

### Fits Tenpack if...

- electricity is treated as **precision-era infrastructure**, not early convenience;
- electric motors/alternators are behind mechanical crafters and precision mechanisms;
- accumulators/wires become visible factory infrastructure;
- oil/fuel remains logistically relevant instead of being replaced by effortless FE loops;
- liquid blaze burners reinforce fuel logistics instead of trivializing superheating;
- the questbook explains electricity as an *industrial bridge*, not a new main tech tree.

### Does not fit if...

- players can craft an electric motor before experiencing normal Create power;
- FE generation/storage becomes hidden one-block power that kills shafts, steam, and diesel;
- another mod provides cheap FE and the electric motor turns it into free Create power;
- accumulators make mobile contraptions/airships self-sufficient too early;
- Tesla coils become cheap faction defense damage blocks;
- biofuel/superheating bypasses intended blaze burner/blaze cake/oil pressure.

## Recommendation

**Tentative yes, but not as a blind add.**

Create Crafts & Additions probably aligns with Tenpack if we add it as a precision-era electricity bridge with explicit invariants:

1. `electric_motor`, `alternator`, and `tesla_coil` must stay mechanical-crafting/precision-era.
2. `portable_energy_interface` should probably require at least a precision mechanism or a tuned brass/fluid component.
3. `modular_accumulator` should probably require precision-era parts if FE storage feels too strong.
4. Liquid blaze burner / straw should be checked against oil and superheat progression.
5. Consider config tuning before deployment:
   - lower `max_stress` if motors feel too compact;
   - keep `generator_efficiency < 1.0`;
   - possibly reduce connector/accumulator transfer if networks feel too strong;
   - possibly tune Tesla Coil player damage or delay its recipe.
6. Add checker rules if C&A is adopted so future recipe updates cannot make the electricity bridge early.

## My current call

Do **not** add C&A in the current rails/deco/food batch. Do a dedicated electricity pass next if wanted.

If added later, I would make it a questbook chapter after precision engineering:

> Electricity lets factories move power between fixed industrial sites, but it does not replace learning shafts, belts, steam, oil, trains, or fuel logistics.
